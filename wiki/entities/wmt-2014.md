# WMT 2014

The **2014 Workshop on Statistical Machine Translation** benchmark — a standard dataset and evaluation for neural machine translation. Two tracks are especially common:

- **WMT 2014 English-German (En-De)** — ~4.5M sentence pairs. `newstest2014` is the standard held-out test set; `newstest2013` is the dev set.
- **WMT 2014 English-French (En-Fr)** — ~36M sentence pairs. Much larger and considered easier in absolute BLEU terms.

These are the two benchmarks on which [[summary-attention-is-all-you-need|Attention Is All You Need]] reports its headline results:

- Transformer (big): **28.4 BLEU** on En-De — new SOTA, +2.0 BLEU over the previous best (which was itself an ensemble).
- Transformer (big): **41.8 BLEU** on En-Fr — new single-model SOTA.
- Training cost for these numbers: 3.5 days on 8× P100 GPUs, a small fraction of the compute used by prior SOTA models.

For roughly a decade after, WMT'14 En-De was the dominant reference benchmark for neural MT research and an obligatory comparison table in nearly every new architecture paper.
