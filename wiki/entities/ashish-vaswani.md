# Ashish Vaswani

Researcher, formerly at [[google-brain|Google Brain]]. First-listed author of [[summary-attention-is-all-you-need|Attention Is All You Need]] (2017) — the paper that introduced the [[transformer]].

Per the author footnote in the paper, Vaswani (with [[illia-polosukhin|Illia Polosukhin]]) "designed and implemented the first Transformer models and has been crucially involved in every aspect of this work." Listing order among the equally-contributing authors is explicitly random — the paper's footnote stresses that all eight authors contributed equally.
