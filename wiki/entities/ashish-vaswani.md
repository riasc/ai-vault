# Ashish Vaswani

## What / Who

Researcher, formerly at [[google-brain|Google Brain]]. First-listed author of [[summary-attention-is-all-you-need|Attention Is All You Need]] (NeurIPS 2017) — the paper that introduced the [[transformer]].

## Significance

Per the author footnote in the paper, Vaswani (with [[illia-polosukhin|Illia Polosukhin]]) "designed and implemented the first Transformer models and has been crucially involved in every aspect of this work." He and Polosukhin are the engineering core of the original Transformer implementation.

Listing order among the eight equally-contributing authors is explicitly random — the paper's footnote stresses that all eight authors contributed equally. Being "first author" is therefore a typographic accident rather than a claim to primacy.

## Timeline / Notable Work

- **2017** — Co-author and first-listed author of *Attention Is All You Need* (NeurIPS). Co-designed and co-implemented the first Transformer models.
