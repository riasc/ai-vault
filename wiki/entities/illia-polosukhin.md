# Illia Polosukhin

## What / Who

Researcher. Co-author of [[summary-attention-is-all-you-need|Attention Is All You Need]] (2017). Work performed while at Google Research.

## Significance

Per the paper's author footnote, Polosukhin (with [[ashish-vaswani|Ashish Vaswani]]) "designed and implemented the first Transformer models." He and Vaswani are the engineering core of the original implementation.

## Timeline / Notable Work

- **2017** — Co-author of *Attention Is All You Need*. Co-designed and co-implemented the first Transformer models.
- **Later** — Co-founded **NEAR Protocol**, a layer-1 blockchain. (Departed from ML research; historically notable as one of the Transformer co-authors who left big-tech ML for other fields.)
