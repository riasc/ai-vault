# Illia Polosukhin

Researcher. Co-author of [[summary-attention-is-all-you-need|Attention Is All You Need]] (2017). Per the paper's author footnote, Polosukhin (with [[ashish-vaswani|Ashish Vaswani]]) "designed and implemented the first Transformer models." Work performed while at Google Research.
