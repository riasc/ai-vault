# Noam Shazeer

Researcher, formerly at [[google-brain|Google Brain]]. Co-author of [[summary-attention-is-all-you-need|Attention Is All You Need]] (2017).

Per the paper's author footnote, Shazeer **proposed [[scaled-dot-product-attention]], [[multi-head-attention]], and the parameter-free position representation** — arguably the three key technical innovations of the paper.

Also known for prior work on conditional computation — co-author of the 2017 "Outrageously Large Neural Networks" paper introducing the sparsely-gated mixture-of-experts layer. Later co-founded Character.AI.
