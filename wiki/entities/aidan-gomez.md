# Aidan N. Gomez

## What / Who

Researcher. Co-author of [[summary-attention-is-all-you-need|Attention Is All You Need]] (2017), listed on the paper with affiliation University of Toronto and the note "Work performed while at [[google-brain|Google Brain]]."

## Significance

Per the author footnote, Gomez (with Lukasz Kaiser) contributed to the design and implementation of **tensor2tensor**, the framework in which the Transformer was trained and evaluated. tensor2tensor replaced the earlier research codebase, "greatly improving results and massively accelerating our research." The engineering lift here is nontrivial: without a workable training framework, the Transformer experiments wouldn't have been runnable at the scale they needed to be.

## Timeline / Notable Work

- **2017** — Co-author of *Attention Is All You Need*. Contributed to the tensor2tensor codebase.
- **Later** — Co-founded **Cohere**, an LLM company focused on enterprise applications.
