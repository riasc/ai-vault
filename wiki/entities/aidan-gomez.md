# Aidan N. Gomez

Researcher. Co-author of [[summary-attention-is-all-you-need|Attention Is All You Need]] (2017). Listed on the paper as affiliated with the University of Toronto, with the note "Work performed while at [[google-brain|Google Brain]]."

Per the paper's author footnote, Gomez (with Lukasz Kaiser) contributed to the design and implementation of tensor2tensor, the codebase used to train and evaluate the Transformer — replacing the earlier research codebase and significantly accelerating the project's experiments.
