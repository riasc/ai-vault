# Google Brain

Google's deep-learning research group. Institutional home of most of the authors of [[summary-attention-is-all-you-need|Attention Is All You Need]] (2017), where the original [[transformer]] was designed and implemented. Several authors are listed as "Google Research" rather than "Google Brain" specifically, reflecting the overlapping organizational structure of Google's AI research at the time.

The paper's training experiments were run on Google infrastructure: a single machine with 8× NVIDIA P100 GPUs, with 12 hours of training for the base model and 3.5 days for the big model.
