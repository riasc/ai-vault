# Patrick D. Hsu

## What / Who

Co-founder and core investigator at [[arc-institute|Arc Institute]], with affiliations at the University of California, Berkeley. **Co-senior author** of [[summary-evo-2|Evo 2]] (Brixi et al., Nature 2026). Corresponding author email: patrick@arcinstitute.org.

## Significance

Patrick Hsu is one of the principal scientific leads of the Evo series at Arc Institute. He brings molecular biology and CRISPR/genome-engineering expertise to the project — the wet-lab side of the Evo 2 work, including the experimental validation of Evo 2-designed chromatin accessibility patterns in mouse and human cells, would have been hard to execute without leadership rooted in molecular biology rather than pure ML.
