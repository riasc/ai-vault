# Michael Poli

## What / Who

Researcher with affiliations at Stanford University, [[arc-institute|Arc Institute]] (Evo 2 Core Team), and **Liquid AI** (San Francisco). Co-author of [[summary-evo-2|Evo 2]] (Brixi et al., Nature 2026). The architectural through-line of the Evo series — the [[hyena-operator|Hyena]] family of operators and the [[striped-hyena-2|StripedHyena]] architectures — traces back to Poli's work.

## Significance

Michael Poli is the principal architect of the convolutional multi-hybrid sequence-modeling direction that underpins Evo 1 and Evo 2. He is first author of:

- **Hyena Hierarchy** (ICML 2023) — introduced the hyena operator as a sub-quadratic alternative to attention.
- **StripedHyena 1** (ICML 2024, "Mechanistic design and scaling of hybrid architectures") — formalized the hybrid hyena+attention approach used in Evo 1.

The systems-level extension to StripedHyena 2 used in Evo 2 is described in a companion paper by Ku et al. (2025, arXiv:2503.01868) on which Poli is also a co-author.

If transformers are the architectural foundation of modern LLMs, Poli's hyena-hybrid line is the most credible alternative for very long sequences — and Evo 2 is its most prominent application.
