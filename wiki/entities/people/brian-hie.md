# Brian L. Hie

## What / Who

Researcher at [[arc-institute|Arc Institute]] and Stanford University. **Co-senior author** of [[summary-evo-2|Evo 2]] (Brixi et al., Nature 2026). Corresponding author email: brianhie@stanford.edu.

## Significance

Brian Hie is one of the principal architects of the Evo series of DNA foundation models. He was a co-author on Evo 1 (Nguyen et al., Science 2024) and is co-senior author on [[evo-2]]. His broader research focuses on the intersection of large-scale machine learning and computational biology — particularly applying language modeling and self-supervised learning to biological sequences.

## Timeline / Notable Work

- **2024** — Co-author on Evo 1 (Nguyen et al., *Science*). First large prokaryotic DNA foundation model.
- **2026** — Co-senior author of [[evo-2]] (Brixi et al., *Nature*). Cross-domain 40B-parameter DNA foundation model.
